About StockQuest Jr.

Our Mission

StockQuest Jr. was created to make learning about the stock market fun and accessible for kids. We believe that financial literacy is a critical skill, and our goal is to teach the basic concepts of investing in a safe, engaging, and simulated environment.

How to Play

You start with $10,000 in virtual cash. Each day, you can check the news, review stock prices, and decide whether to buy or sell shares in different companies. Your goal is to grow your wealth over 20 days by making smart investment choices. Watch out for market events that can help or hurt your portfolio!
